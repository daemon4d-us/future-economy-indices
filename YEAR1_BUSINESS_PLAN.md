# Future Economy Indices - Year 1 Business Plan ($150K ARR Target)

## Executive Summary

Launch two premium financial newsletter indices (AIINFRA & SPACEINFRA) targeting sophisticated retail investors and finance professionals. Revenue model: subscription-based newsletter with freemium conversion strategy.

## Revenue Model Breakdown

### Pricing Tiers
- **Free Tier**: Monthly index updates, basic commentary
- **Premium Tier**: $29/month or $290/year (17% discount)
- **Professional Tier**: $79/month or $790/year (17% discount)

### Year 1 Revenue Target: $150K ARR

**Conservative Mix Strategy:**
- 400 Premium annual subscribers @ $290 = $116K
- 50 Professional annual subscribers @ $790 = $39.5K
- **Total: $155.5K ARR**

**Alternative Monthly Mix (Year-end state):**
- 250 Premium monthly @ $29 = $87K ARR
- 100 Premium annual @ $290 = $29K ARR
- 30 Professional annual @ $790 = $23.7K ARR
- **Total: $139.7K ARR** (on track to exceed $150K with Q4 growth)

- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Target <$50
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Target >$500

**Net Profit Target: $118K (79% margin)**

## Risk Mitigation

**Market Risk:**
- Diversify content beyond pure stock picks
- Emphasize educational value
- Build community around thematic investing

**Competitive Risk:**
- Focus on niche (infrastructure plays, not general AI/space)
- Emphasize transparent methodology
- Build personal brand/authority

**Churn Risk:**
- Quarterly rebalancing creates natural retention points
- Annual subscriptions reduce monthly churn exposure
- Continuous content delivery maintains engagement

## Success Factors

1. **Consistency**: Never miss a scheduled publication
2. **Transparency**: Full methodology disclosure builds trust
3. **Performance**: Indices must demonstrate defensible returns
4. **Engagement**: Respond to subscribers, build community
5. **Quality**: High-quality research and writing
6. **Distribution**: Multi-channel presence maximizes reach
